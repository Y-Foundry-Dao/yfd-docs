Proposal
========

From here on out, every stage of the process takes place on-chain.

![](/assets/images/figure/proposal-process.png)

Steps that take place during this stage:

-   Submit on-chain proposal

    -   Meet requirements

-   Voting commences

    -   fYFD holders vote

    -   Vote Outcomes

-   Poll Expiry Reached
